# Pregnant Tracker - Baby Bump

## Purpose

Pregnant Tracker - Baby Bump is a wellness-focused mobile app designed to help expecting mothers stay healthy, relaxed, and confident throughout every stage of pregnancy. It combines cycle tracking, pregnancy progress monitoring, nutrition management, and mindfulness tools in a single private companion that keeps all data on-device.

## Core Features

- **Reproductive Health & Cycle Tracking** — Menstrual tracking, ovulation predictions, and fertile window estimates. Log irregular cycles and receive reminders to understand reproductive rhythm
- **Weight & Nutrition Management** — Track pregnancy weight changes with simple charts and daily logs. Nutrition and hydration tips tailored for each pregnancy stage
- **Pregnancy Progress & Self-Care** — Due date calculation, weekly updates on body changes, guidance on safe exercises, kegel routines, and daily self-care reminders
- **Stress Relief & Mindfulness** — Breathing tips, light stretching, and mindful activities for managing stress and emotional changes naturally
- **Health Organization & Daily Management** — Note doctor visits, track symptoms, and save reminders for upcoming checkups in a single secure place
- **Emotional & Behavioral Wellness** — Log moods, energy levels, and symptoms to understand emotional patterns. Gentle recommendations for relaxation and positivity
- **Educational Health Content** — Trusted educational resources about maternal care, pregnancy nutrition, and body changes with medically reviewed, easy-to-understand content

## Target Audience

- **First-Time Expecting Mothers** — Need guidance on what to expect at each stage, want reassurance and educational content
- **Health-Conscious Mothers** — Track weight, nutrition, and exercise throughout pregnancy, value data and progress charts
- **Anxiety-Prone Mothers** — Benefit from mindfulness tools, breathing exercises, and mood tracking to manage pregnancy-related stress

## Visual Direction

- Soft, nurturing pastel palette with lavender and blush tones
- Warm and calming atmosphere that conveys care and safety
- Illustrated fetal-development scenes and fruit-size baby comparisons
- Spacious layout with generous whitespace for emotional calm
- Gentle spring-based animations that reinforce a sense of softness

## Technical Requirements

- Flutter with Material 3 theming
- Offline-first with all data stored on-device for privacy
- Mobile-only (portrait, phone-optimized)
- Smooth 60fps animations and chart transitions
- Accessible (screen reader support, dynamic type, reduced motion)
