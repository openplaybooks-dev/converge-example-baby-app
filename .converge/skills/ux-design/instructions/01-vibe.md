# Vibe Definition

The vibe is the emotional and visual personality of the app. It drives every design decision.

## Core Identity
Define 3-4 adjectives that capture the app's personality:
- **Clean** — Minimal clutter, generous whitespace, clear hierarchy
- **Screenshot-beautiful** — Every screen could be a portfolio piece
- **Adorable** — Delightful micro-interactions, warm personality
- **Modern** — Contemporary patterns, current design trends

## Vibe Dimensions
Rate each on a spectrum:
- **Temperature**: Cool ←→ Warm
- **Density**: Spacious ←→ Dense
- **Formality**: Casual ←→ Professional
- **Motion**: Still ←→ Animated
- **Depth**: Flat ←→ Layered
